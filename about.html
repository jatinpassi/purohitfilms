About Us

We have the pleasure of introducing our company supported with a team of creative professionals of high caliber dedicated to carve a niche in the Media and its diversified sectors.

Purohit Films is prominently into the Electronic Media Programming producing content for top Radio & TV channels of the country. We produced a series entitled ‘MERA DOST’ aired on National Channel of Doordarshan (DD-I) at 1:00pm on every Sunday, fetching high TRP in children category. Before this ‘SCHOOL SCHOOL’ has been one of the most popular program aired on DD-I in the children category from our production house.

Besides, Television series, Purohit films is producing Commercials/Audio-visuals, Public Service Films, Quiz shows, Documentaries, Print Ads, Radio commercials/Jingles etc. We have the efficient and vigorous technical team with fully equipped editing studio to execute any job under taken.

Purohit Films is an Empanelled production company with DAVP, Ministry of HRD, Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Ministry of Agriculture and some of the prime TV channels & institutions of India. Purohit Films has also been working with N.G.O. to create awareness in social cause through Radio and Television promotion.

Purohit Films is producing its first feature addressing the adolescent and youth of the country.

  Should you require more information? We shall be too happy to meet you.
